# New Employee Onboarding Guide — TechNova Inc.

Welcome to TechNova! This guide will help you get started during your first week.

## Day 1 — Setup

- [ ] Receive your laptop and set up your accounts (email, Slack, GitHub).
- [ ] Complete your I-9 and tax forms with HR.
- [ ] Get your access badge from the security desk (Building A, 1st floor).
- [ ] Attend the Welcome Orientation session at 10:00 AM in Conference Room B.

## Day 2 — Meet Your Team

- [ ] 1-on-1 with your direct manager to discuss your first project.
- [ ] Meet your team members and learn about ongoing projects.
- [ ] Set up your development environment (see Engineering wiki if applicable).

## Day 3 — Training

- [ ] Complete mandatory compliance training modules in the HR portal:
  - Anti-Harassment Training (45 min)
  - Data Security Awareness (30 min)
  - Code of Conduct Review (15 min)

## Day 4-5 — Get Started

- [ ] Review your department's documentation and current projects.
- [ ] Attend any relevant team standups or planning meetings.
- [ ] Schedule coffee chats with 2-3 colleagues from other teams.
