## Purpose

This Code of Conduct defines the standards of behavior expected from all employees at TechNova Inc. Our goal is to maintain a respectful, inclusive, and productive workplace.

## Core Values

1. **Integrity:** Be honest in all communications and dealings.
2. **Respect:** Treat every colleague, partner, and customer with dignity.
3. **Collaboration:** Work together across teams and departments.
4. **Innovation:** Challenge the status quo and suggest improvements.

## Workplace Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated. This includes behavior based on race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic.
- All employees must complete annual anti-harassment training.
- Conflicts should be resolved through direct, respectful conversation. If unresolved, escalate to your manager or HR.

## Communication Standards

- Use professional language in all workplace communications (Slack, email, meetings).
- Respond to messages from colleagues within one business day.
- When disagreeing, focus on ideas, not individuals.

## Confidentiality

- Do not share proprietary company information with external parties unless authorized.
- Client data must be handled in accordance with our data protection policy.
- Access to sensitive systems should follow the principle of least privilege.

## Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience a violation of this code, report it to:
- Your direct manager
- The HR department (hr@technova.com)
- The anonymous ethics hotline: 1-800-555-ETHICS

All reports will be investigated promptly and confidentially. Retaliation against anyone who reports a concern in good faith is strictly prohibited.
