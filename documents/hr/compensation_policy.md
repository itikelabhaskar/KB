# Compensation Policy — TechNova Inc.

**CONFIDENTIAL — HR Department Only**

This document contains sensitive compensation information and is restricted to authorized HR personnel and company administrators.

## Salary Bands (Annual, USD)

| Level | Title | Min | Mid | Max |
|-------|-------|-----|-----|-----|
| L1 | Junior Engineer / Associate | $65,000 | $80,000 | $95,000 |
| L2 | Engineer / Specialist | $90,000 | $110,000 | $130,000 |
| L3 | Senior Engineer / Sr. Specialist | $120,000 | $145,000 | $170,000 |
| L4 | Staff Engineer / Manager | $155,000 | $180,000 | $210,000 |
| L5 | Principal / Director | $190,000 | $230,000 | $280,000 |

## Annual Bonus Structure

- **Individual Performance:** Up to 15% of base salary based on annual review score.
- **Company Performance:** Up to 10% of base salary based on company revenue targets.
- **Bonus payout schedule:** March of the following year.

## Equity Program

- New hires at L3 and above receive stock options vesting over 4 years with a 1-year cliff.
- Refresh grants are considered annually during the compensation review cycle.

## Salary Review Cycle

Salary reviews happen annually in January. Adjustments are based on:
1. Market data (we target the 75th percentile for each role).
2. Individual performance rating from the annual review.
3. Internal equity across the team.

## Promotion Criteria

Promotions require:
- At least 1 year in current level
- Manager nomination
- Calibration committee approval
- Demonstration of next-level competencies for at least 2 quarters
