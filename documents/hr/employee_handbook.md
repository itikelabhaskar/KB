## Company Overview

TechNova Inc. is a technology company founded in 2018, specializing in cloud-based enterprise solutions. We currently have 250 employees across three main departments: Human Resources, Engineering, and Sales.

## Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

All full-time employees receive **20 days of PTO per year**, accrued at 1.67 days per month. PTO can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 unused days. Any days beyond the 5-day carryover limit will expire on December 31st.

### How to Request PTO

1. Submit your request through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance.
2. Your manager will approve or deny the request within 3 business days.
3. For requests of 5 or more consecutive days, submit at least 30 days in advance.

## Sick Leave

Employees receive **10 days of sick leave per year**, separate from PTO. Sick leave does not carry over. If you are sick for more than 3 consecutive days, you may need to provide a doctor's note upon return.

## Remote Work Policy

TechNova supports a hybrid work model:

- All employees may work remotely **up to 3 days per week**.
- You must be in the office on **Tuesday and Thursday** (core collaboration days).
- Remote work arrangements must be coordinated with your direct manager.
- Employees working remotely must be available during core hours: **10:00 AM to 4:00 PM** in their local time zone.

## Benefits

- **Health Insurance:** Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage for employees and dependents.
- **401(k):** Company matches up to 4% of your salary.
- **Professional Development:** $2,000 annual budget per employee for courses, conferences, or certifications.
- **Parental Leave:** 12 weeks paid leave for primary caregivers, 4 weeks for secondary caregivers.

## Office Hours and Holidays

Standard office hours are **9:00 AM to 6:00 PM**, Monday through Friday. TechNova observes the following holidays:

- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (Thursday and Friday)
- Christmas Eve and Christmas Day
- New Year's Eve (half day)
