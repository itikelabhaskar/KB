TechNova offers three pricing tiers designed for teams of different sizes and needs.

## Starter — Best for Small Teams

Perfect for startups and small teams getting started. Includes all core features: document management, basic search, and team collaboration. No credit card required for the 14-day free trial.

## Pro — Best for Growing Companies

Everything in Starter, plus advanced features like SSO integration, detailed audit logs, priority support, and higher API limits. Most popular among mid-size companies with 20-80 users.

## Enterprise — Best for Large Organizations

Fully customized solution with unlimited everything. Includes dedicated account management, custom integrations (Salesforce, SAP, Workday), on-premise deployment option, and a 99.99% SLA.

### Enterprise Add-ons

- **Data Residency:** Choose your data center region (US, EU, APAC).
- **Advanced Analytics:** Custom dashboards and usage reports.
- **Professional Services:** Implementation support and training.

## Discounts

- **Annual billing:** 20% discount on all tiers.
- **Nonprofit organizations:** 50% discount on Pro and Enterprise.
- **Startups (<2 years old, <20 employees):** 30% discount on Pro for the first year.
